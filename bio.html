Who am I?

Born on April 3rd, 2000, I am a millennium Aries baby, if that’s a thing. I grew an only child on the north side of Charlotte, N.C. in an area called Hornets Nest Park and have been living here ever since. Growing up with a passion for technology and building things, you would always catch me with a phone or a handheld video game, maybe even taking apart someones else’s phone from time to time. I’ve literally had my career path set on technology since I was about nine years old and always knew that one day I would be working with it, doing something I love! I attended Phillip O. Berry Academy of Technology where I participated in the Engineering Academy and received my NAF diploma along with my actual high school diploma. I have yet to go to college because I am currently participating in the Road to Hire program at Red Ventures and I am training to become a Web Developer soon. Some of my hobbies include playing video games, watching YouTube videos, and watching and spectating on football and basketball games.

Why Tech?

As I mentioned before, my passion for technology started when I was young. I didn’t have much of it coming up but as I got older and realized the great impact it had on our world and our society it kinda just grew on me. From education to health science, technology plays a big part in our lives and the endless creativity it brings out makes it 10x more fun! Technology has taken a big leap since I was younger and I would love to learn as much as I can about it so I can then help the newer generations out by making their lives much easier, organized and safer. Honestly without technology I don’t know where I would be in my journey today…
